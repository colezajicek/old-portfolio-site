Pantone 448c

Hex: #4A412A

RGB: 74, 65, 42

Variations:

Opacity 0.5

This is Pantone 448c. Oft maligned, I find it to be a lovely color. It's negative descriptions only serve to increase my joy towards this color. On this site it plays a large role at the moment, though as time goes on it may be relegated to the pets page as I find that its use captures the combination of "zoo animal description plaque" and "colonial British explorer" aesthetic perfectly
