Previous pets

The Snakes

The snakes were a paradigm in the early '20's. The snakes, with the exception of the Kingsnakes, were rescues or rehomes from other owners. Snake Haus was the local shelter that I became familiar with and became the focus of my philanthropy efforts for those years. The snakes became a reality immediately preceding moving into our own house. The phase was likely due to The Vanishing Snake incident. The snakes were all eventually rehomed before moving cross country. while this was hard at the time, it turned out to be a good idea because we were stranded in Arizona for 2 weeks during our move and because of the circumstances, the physical stress may well have killed several of them. Instead we wished them well into several experienced reptile homes and rescues.
